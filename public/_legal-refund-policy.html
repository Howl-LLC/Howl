Howl Refund Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026 · Last Updated: April 20, 2026

We want you to be happy with your Howl purchase. If something isn't right, you can request a refund under the terms below.

1. Overview

Howl offers a one-time refund per purchase category within 5 calendar days of the original charge. Refunds are processed through Stripe and returned to your original payment method in the currency of the original charge, including any tax originally collected. Consumer-protection law in your jurisdiction may grant additional rights (see Section 4).

2. Eligible Purchases

Refunds may be requested for the following categories. Each category allows one lifetime refund per Howl account:

  • Subscription charges: Monthly charges for Howl Pro Essential or Howl Pro plans.
  • Gift subscription purchases: One-time payments for gift subscriptions, only if the gift has not yet been redeemed by the recipient.
  • Power-Up charges: Monthly charges for purchased Power-Up slots.

3. Refund Window

Refund requests must be submitted within 5 calendar days of the charge date. Charges older than 5 days are not eligible for self-service refund. See Section 4 for EU, EEA, and UK consumers.

4. EU, EEA, and UK Consumer Withdrawal

If you are a consumer resident in the European Union, the European Economic Area, or the United Kingdom, you have a 14-day right of withdrawal for digital services purchased through Howl under applicable consumer-protection law (including the EU Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU and equivalent UK law).

By starting to use a paid feature immediately on purchase, you consent to the service beginning before the end of the 14-day withdrawal period. Under Article 16(m) of the EU Consumer Rights Directive, your right of withdrawal is lost once the service has been fully performed during this period with your prior express consent and acknowledgement. For subscription services that are not fully performed (for example, a multi-month subscription only partially consumed), the 14-day withdrawal right remains available.

EU, EEA, and UK consumers who wish to exercise the 14-day withdrawal right should contact support@howlpro.com with the subject line "EU Withdrawal".

5. Auto-Renewal Terms

Subscriptions and Power-Up slots renew automatically at the frequency and price disclosed at the point of purchase until you cancel. By purchasing a subscription you consent to these recurring charges.

  • Cancel anytime. You can cancel at any time through Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription without contacting support. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; no further charges will be made.
  • Renewal reminders. Where required by law (for example, under California Business and Professions Code § 17600 et seq. and similar laws in New York, Connecticut, Illinois, the District of Columbia, and other jurisdictions), we send a renewal reminder before each renewal charge.
  • Price changes. We will provide advance notice of any change to subscription pricing before it takes effect on a future renewal. You may cancel before the next renewal to avoid the new price.
  • Method of acceptance. These auto-renewal terms were presented to you at the time of purchase and you agreed to them as part of completing the transaction.

6. One-Time Limit

Each Howl account may use one refund per category over the lifetime of the account. For example, you may request one subscription refund and one gift refund, but you cannot request a second subscription refund. This limit exists to prevent abuse and keep costs low for everyone.

7. How to Request a Refund

Eligible refunds can be requested directly from the Howl app:

  • Open Settings → Billing.
  • If you have an eligible charge, a Request Refund button will appear.
  • Confirm your request. Eligible refunds are processed via Stripe.

Refunds typically appear on your statement within 5 to 10 business days after processing, depending on your bank or card issuer. International and cross-currency refunds may take longer.

8. What Happens After a Refund

8.1 Subscription Refund

Your subscription is immediately canceled and your account is downgraded to Free. Premium cosmetics (custom name color, name effects, avatar effects, banner image, and custom background) are removed. Your discriminator may be reassigned.

8.2 Gift Refund

The payment is reversed and the gift code is invalidated. This is only available if the recipient has not yet claimed the gift.

8.3 Power-Up Refund

Your Power-Up subscription is immediately canceled. If your deployed Power-Ups exceed your remaining free allowance, excess Power-Ups may be revoked from the servers they are applied to.

9. Non-Refundable Purchases

  • Charges older than 5 calendar days (except where EU, EEA, or UK withdrawal rights apply under Section 4).
  • Second refund requests for a category where a refund has already been issued.
  • Gift subscriptions that have already been redeemed by the recipient.
  • Voluntary donations.
  • Pro-rated refunds: We do not issue pro-rated refunds for unused portions of a billing period. If you cancel mid-period, your subscription remains active until the end of the period, after which it expires.
  • App-store purchases: Purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play are handled under the refund policy of the platform used to make the purchase. Contact Apple or Google directly to request a refund for those purchases.

10. Chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us at support@howlpro.com before disputing with your card issuer. Chargebacks submitted to your card issuer in lieu of contacting us may result in account suspension pending resolution and forfeiture of refund rights for that charge.

11. Failed Payments

If a subscription renewal payment fails, we retry the payment for up to 7 days before downgrading the account to Free. Cosmetics, Power-Ups, and other subscription features may be suspended or removed during the retry period. Resolving the payment issue within this window restores the subscription without interruption to your account.

12. Exceptions and Disputes

If your situation is not covered by the self-service refund system (for example, a billing error, unauthorized charge, or extraordinary circumstances), contact us at support@howlpro.com. For disputes, we may request your Stripe receipt, subscription ID, or other proof of purchase to verify your request. Howl reserves the right to issue additional refunds at its sole discretion on a case-by-case basis.

13. Cancellation Without Refund

If you wish to cancel your subscription without requesting a refund, you can do so through Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period, and you will not be charged again.

14. Contact

For questions about this policy or your refund eligibility, contact us at support@howlpro.com.
